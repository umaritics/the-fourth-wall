About Us

Since 2012, The Fourth Wall has given voice to emerging artists—curating fearless, joy-filled art that brings colour and character into homes worldwide.

Our goal is to present opportunities to buyers at a range of price points to complete their spaces with beautiful original artworks. Whether you are searching for something to your tastes under $500, or you have the means to purchase works valued above $1,000, The Fourth Wall is where you will find the pieces you are looking for.
